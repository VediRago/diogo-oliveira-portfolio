# Part 01 — Rutare's Confession

These are the recollections of my past.

What follows was never meant to fall into others' eyes.

The history of House Ventari is tainted. We forced gratitude and took advantage of the grateful. We sowed pride and fed it with lies, false honor, imposed morality, and plain deceit.

This is the weight I carry every day: knowing that everything supporting our current civilization was built on greed and a lust for power.

I am Rutare Ventari, rightful heir of the Ventari line, though that right was stripped from me by brothers carrying the same hunger our father once had.

It was not always like this. We were once a proud noble line. But as you will see, nothing lasts forever.

Shame is all I feel as I lie upon my deathbed, watching my descendants carry a banner of lies, praising a history they do not understand and shouting the name of a man our house used, spent, and crowned itself with.

Viriatus.
