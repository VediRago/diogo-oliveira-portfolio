- Designing controlled information flow

- Testing how presentation changes interpretation

## Worldbuilding

- Building lore from people, pressure, history, and consequence
- Connecting family behavior, belief, institutions, and social pressure
- Designing factions and locations around inherited wounds and practical needs
- Avoiding disconnected lore by tying details to behavior and conflict
- Documenting world logic clearly enough to support future quests and NPC behavior
