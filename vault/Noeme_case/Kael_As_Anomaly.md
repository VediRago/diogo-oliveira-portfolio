# Kael as Anomaly

← Back to [Cosmology Reference](Cosmology_Reference.md)

Kael becomes an anomaly when the system can no longer classify or resolve her correctly.

Her anomalous state comes from the interaction between her psychic nature, displacement exposure, later human augmentation, and the Unity event involving Erit.

After augmentation, Kael no longer fits the normal [Resolution](Resolution.md) pathway. Her condition does not resolve as expected because [Qalm](Qalm.md) no longer tracks her correctly.

Kael is not chosen. She becomes impossible to classify.

That makes her the first observable anomaly. The consequences spread through [Kael Quarantine and Contamination](Kael_Quarantine_And_Contamination.md).
