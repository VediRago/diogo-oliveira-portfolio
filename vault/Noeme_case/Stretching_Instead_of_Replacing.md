# Stretching Instead of Replacing

A weaker mystery structure replaces answers:

```text
Answer A
↓
Actually Answer B
```

That can make earlier attention feel wasted.

Noeme uses stretching instead.

```text
Answer A
↓
New evidence
↓
Answer A inside a larger context
```

The first explanation remains meaningful. It was not wrong. It was too small.

## How It Works

A first answer should work at the scale where the audience finds it.

Later evidence should not delete that answer. It should make the audience expand their model.

This creates discovery without making the reveal feel like a trick.

## Virus and Condition

The virus is a useful example.

Early symptoms make a biological explanation feel reasonable. The virus exists. It causes real damage. It affects the world and the characters.

Later, more evidence appears where infection alone does not explain the pattern.

The virus remains real.

The pattern is larger.

The audience was right to notice the symptoms. What changed was the scale of the answer.
