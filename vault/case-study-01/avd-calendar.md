# AVD Calendar

AVD means **After Viriatus's Death**.

The name suggests that the calendar begins when [Viriatus](viriatus.md) dies, but that is not the full truth. Year 0 AVD begins when [Regulatus](regulatus.md) is publicly declared.

That difference matters because Erit's calendar does not begin with the private truth. It begins when [House Ventari](house-ventari.md) successfully controls what Viriatus's death means.

The private truth is murder.

The public story is noble death in war.

The calendar begins when the public story wins.

Through the AVD calendar, Viriatus's death becomes more than memory. It becomes the starting point of history, law, ceremony, records, and civic identity.

To live inside AVD time is to live inside the meaning [Ermerus Ventari](ermerus-ventari.md) created from Viriatus's death.

This makes the calendar one of the clearest examples of House Ventari's power: it controls events and shapes how those events are remembered.
