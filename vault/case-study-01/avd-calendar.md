# AVD Calendar

AVD means **After Viriatus's Death**.

The name suggests that the calendar begins when [Viriatus](viriatus.md) dies, but that is not the full truth. Year 0 AVD begins when [Regulatus](regulatus.md) is publicly declared.

That difference matters because Erit's calendar does not begin with the private truth. It begins when [House Ventari](house-ventari.md) successfully controls what Viriatus's death means.

The private truth is murder. The public story is noble death in war. The calendar begins when the public story wins.

Through the AVD calendar, Viriatus's death becomes more than memory. It becomes the starting point of history, law, ceremony, records, and civic identity. To live inside AVD time is to live inside the meaning [Ermerus Ventari](ermerus-ventari.md) created from that death.

The calendar spans five eras. The Founding Era establishes Regulatus and the early empire. By around 1000 AVD, Vys has grown into a metropolis and the world enters an industrial age. Cybernetics arrive in two stages: external prosthetics around 2000 AVD, then [Devaar](house-devaar.md)'s controlled medical infrastructure around 2330 AVD. A solar flare famine around 2500 AVD accelerates dependency on those systems. The playable Dark Time begins around 2600 AVD. Era Five, Erit's far-future era, begins after 3000 AVD.

This makes the AVD calendar one of the clearest examples of House Ventari's power: it records history, shapes how history is remembered, and measures everything that follows from the moment their story won.
