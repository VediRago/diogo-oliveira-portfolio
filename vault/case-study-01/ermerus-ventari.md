# Ermerus Ventari

Ermerus Ventari is the last son of [House Ventari](house-ventari.md).

Where [Ilunis Ventari](ilunis-ventari.md) creates power through force and [Rutare Ventari](rutare-ventari.md) through procedure, Ermerus creates power by controlling what people need to believe. He grows in the space left between his siblings — charming, socially fluent, and difficult to target. His danger is interpretation.

When [Ecalus Ventari](ecalus-ventari.md) dies, [Viriatus](viriatus.md) becomes more than a military asset — he becomes a public symbol strong enough to outrank the blood heirs of House Ventari. Ermerus's solution is not to defeat that symbol but to redirect it. He approaches Ilunis not with a command but with a story: that Viriatus, beloved by their father above his own maimed son, is not their blood, and that removing him is protecting the family. He turns jealousy into duty, and Ilunis acts.

After the murder, Ermerus controls the aftermath — framing the Servan witnesses as thieves, having them killed, and rewriting a private killing during mourning into a noble death in war. From the public grief that follows, he builds [Regulatus](regulatus.md). A living Viriatus could have become the center of the new order. A dead one becomes the foundation of an order Ermerus interprets.

The same blood logic he used to remove Viriatus later returns against his own line. After his death, disputed children outside the secure Ventari bloodline are stripped of their claim by Ilunis's descendants. From that rejection, [Novacula](novacula.md) is born.
