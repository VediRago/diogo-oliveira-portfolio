# Ermerus Ventari

Ermerus Ventari is the last son of [House Ventari](house-ventari.md).

Unlike [Ilunis Ventari](ilunis-ventari.md), who creates power through force, or [Rutare Ventari](rutare-ventari.md), who creates power through order and procedure, Ermerus creates power by understanding what people need to believe.

Because he is not the obvious heir, weapon, or scholar, Ermerus grows in the space left between his siblings. He becomes charming, socially fluent, and difficult to target directly. His danger is interpretation.

Ermerus understands that people obey the story that makes force feel necessary.

After [Ecalus Ventari](ecalus-ventari.md) dies, [Viriatus](viriatus.md) becomes more than a military advantage. He becomes a public symbol strong enough to outrank the blood heirs of House Ventari.

Ermerus does not need to destroy Viriatus's meaning. He needs to redirect it. He gives Ilunis a story where killing Viriatus feels like family duty rather than jealousy, turning the wound around **blood** into a reason to act.

After Viriatus is murdered, Ermerus controls the aftermath, hides the truth, removes witnesses, and turns a private killing into a public noble death. From that public grief, he creates [Regulatus](regulatus.md).

Ermerus creates the first era by controlling Viriatus's death and turning public grief into Regulatus, but the freedom that gives him charm and influence also leaves behind disputed children outside the secure Ventari line.

After Ermerus dies, Ilunis's descendants use blood legitimacy to remove those children and their claim from House Ventari. From that rejection, [Novacula](novacula.md) is born.
