# Viriatus

Viriatus is used in Case Study 02 as the character-level example for the modular method.

His story begins with experience: displacement into a world he does not understand, discovery by Servan, and adoption into House Ventari. That experience creates fear, longing, and a need for belonging.

Those emotions become a reason: order can protect what chaos destroys, and legacy must survive through something larger than the self.

That reason becomes behavior. Viriatus accepts training, becomes useful, releases pressure through conquest, and eventually becomes a figure people love beyond House Ventari's control.

For the method, Viriatus shows that a character does not need to begin as quest logic. He can begin with the base chain: experience, emotion, reason, and behavior.
