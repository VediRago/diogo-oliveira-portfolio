# 2026-06-04 — Erit World Development Diary

## Main Purpose

Today I began building Erit's world as a support structure for Fragments.

The goal was to test whether existing story details could grow into a deeper worldbuilding system without damaging the main story.

The working rule became:

> Story first. Systems after.

Erit's world should come from details already present in Fragments: Erit, the Devaar family, augmentation, inherited power, displacement, and the statue seen by Sera.

## Main Discovery

The first practical question was:

> How do I make it all fit?

The answer was to build a fit map from existing story details.

```text
Sera sees statue
-> statue belongs to displaced founder figure
-> founder reshaped Rome through Regulatus
-> Regulatus created long-term obsession with order, continuity, inheritance, and controlled development
-> dynastic lines formed
-> Devaar grew from one of those lines through augmentation and preservation knowledge
-> Erit exists inside that inherited system
-> Sera and Kael entering that world creates conflict with identity, medicine, power, and trust
```

This became the first support chain for Erit's world.

## Key Developments

### Displaced Founder

The world needed a person before it needed a map.

The displaced child became the foundation node: a human from a more advanced civilization entering Roman history and being interpreted through myth, prophecy, politics, and divine legitimacy.

This made the divergence personal instead of abstract.

### Calendar Origin

A calendar problem revealed a deeper political foundation.

```text
calendar need
-> AD
-> whose death matters enough to reset time?
-> the statue
-> founder figure
-> later Viriatus
-> House Ventari turns his death into Regulatus
```

The calendar became propaganda: one date with different meanings depending on who reads it.

### Regulatus

Regulatus became clearer as emotional control.

It teaches people that obedience means worth, gratitude means maturity, and accepting their place means protecting the whole.

From the inside, it feels like pride, belonging, family honor, safety, purpose, and identity.

This makes Regulatus useful for NPC behavior because characters can speak and act from the system they were raised inside.

### Founding Lines

The dynastic structure became four founding lines:

- Ermerus
- Rutare
- Ilunis
- Teresta

Their initials form **ERIT**.

The line method became:

```text
experience -> emotional wound -> behavior -> historical path -> modern system
```

Each line should begin with a person and an emotional wound, then develop into a family pattern, symbol, historical path, and modern system.

## Constraints Found

Displacement can exist in Erit's world history, but it cannot be common.

```text
Displacement exists, but history never has enough clean cases to turn it into science.
```

The founder can reinforce displacement as a historical wound, but he cannot explain Unity or replace Sera, Kael, or Erit.

Final constraint:

```text
Build only what creates story pressure, character behavior, social conflict, or player-facing choices.
```
