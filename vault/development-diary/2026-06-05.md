# 2026-06-05 — Erit World Development Diary

## Main Purpose

Today focused on strengthening the foundation created on 2026-06-04.

The goal was not to add new lore, but to make the existing causal chain clearer:

```text
statue
-> founder
-> calendar
-> Viriatus
-> House Ventari
-> Regulatus
-> Empiran order
-> founding lines
-> House Devaar
-> NPC and quest pressure
```

## Main Discovery

The world became more useful when power was traced through people instead of institutions.

House Ventari became the family that conceals Viriatus's arrival, shapes him into a conqueror, removes him when he becomes too influential, and formalizes Regulatus to claim his victories as inherited legitimacy.

This clarified the origin of power and connected Viriatus, Regulatus, and the later Empiran order into a single chain.

## Key Developments

### House Ventari

House Ventari became the foundation of the ruling order.

Their claim is not:

> We found him.

It is:

> We formed him.

This becomes the justification for inheriting everything that follows.

### Viriatus

The founder's official mythic name became **Viriatus**.

The founder, the statue, the calendar, and the sacred death narrative were connected into the same historical figure.

### House Devaar

House Devaar became the later dominant family.

Where House Ventari controls ideology, House Devaar gains influence through systems people depend on:

- preservation medicine
- augmentation
- bodily identification
- treatment access

This makes House Devaar feel like a historical consequence rather than an isolated powerful family.

### NPC Relationship Model

A new NPC framework was created:

```text
System Relationship
-> Location Pressure
-> Group Pressure
-> Personal State
```

NPCs do not experience Regulatus in the same way.

Possible relationships include:

- Believer
- Rejector
- Damaged Believer
- Outsider
- Performer / Manipulator

This creates more varied social behavior while preserving the same underlying system.

## Design Takeaway

The world became more useful once institutions were traced back to people, motives, and historical decisions.

The guiding principle remained:

```text
A story creates pressure.
Repeated details become clues.
Clues become systems.
Systems create new paths without breaking the core story.
```
