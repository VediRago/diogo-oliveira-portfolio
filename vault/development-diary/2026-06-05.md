# 2026-06-05 — Erit World Development Diary

## Main Purpose

Today focused on strengthening the foundation created on 2026-06-04.

The goal was to make the existing causal chain clearer, from the statue and founder to Viriatus, House Ventari, Regulatus, House Devaar, and later NPC or quest pressure.

## Main Focus Areas

### House Ventari

House Ventari became the family at the center of the ruling order.

Their power comes from shaping Viriatus, controlling his public meaning, and using his victories as proof that their discipline can create order.

Their claim is not that they found him.

Their claim is that they formed him.

### Viriatus

The displaced founder's official mythic name became **Viriatus**.

The founder, the statue, the calendar, and the sacred death narrative were connected into the same historical figure.

### House Devaar

House Devaar became the later dominant family by controlling systems people physically depend on.

Its direction moved toward preservation medicine, augmentation, bodily identification, treatment access, and dependency through medicine.

This made House Devaar feel like a historical consequence rather than a random powerful family.

### NPC Relationship Model

A new NPC framework was created:

```text
System Relationship
-> Location Pressure
-> Group Pressure
-> Personal State
```

This allowed different characters to relate to Regulatus in different ways while still being shaped by the same system.

### Common Terms

A common terminology file was created to prevent naming drift.

This helped stabilize names such as Viriatus, House Ventari, House Devaar, Regulatus, Empiran, and the Roman-derived terms used across the world.

### Quest Pressure

The worldbuilding began connecting more clearly to future quest design.

The focus moved from lore alone toward systems that could create citizenship conflict, medical access problems, augmentation pressure, class pressure, and rebellion.

## Design Takeaway

The world became stronger once institutions were traced back to people, motives, and historical decisions.

The guiding chain remained:

```text
A story creates pressure.
Repeated details become clues.
Clues become systems.
Systems create new paths without breaking the core story.
```
