# 2026-06-06 — Erit World Development Diary

## Main Purpose

Today focused on organizing the Erit worldbuilding notes before continuing development.

The goal was to make the material easier to read, expand, and reference without losing the causal chain behind the world.

## Main Focus Areas

### Note Structure

The main notes were reorganized so the Erit material would be easier to navigate and continue.

This helped separate broad structure, history, diary notes, and active worldbuilding work.

### Development Diary Split

The development diary was split into dated entries.

This made the design trail easier to follow and stopped the diary from becoming one large combined file.

### Era Structure

The world was organized around a clearer historical progression:

```text
Founding -> Fracture -> Devaar Rise -> Erit Era
```

This gave each era a purpose instead of forcing every idea into the same time period.

### Calendar And Timeline Split

The calendar and timeline were separated.

The calendar explains how time is counted and why AVD matters.

The timeline explains what happens inside that structure.

### Founding Era Cleanup

The Founding Era was tightened around Viriatus, House Ventari, Ecalus, and the creation of Regulatus.

The main focus was making the private truth and the public story easier to separate.

### Fracture Era Outline

The Fracture Era began taking shape as the first major consequence of Regulatus.

Its focus became city pressure, rebellion, family fracture, and the early formation of institutions.

### City Pressure Foundations

The first major city roles were clarified.

Vys became the center of authority, Lucrest became the strongest rebel pressure, and Arumis became the port, mining, academic, and metallurgical future.

### Faction Triangle

Three major forces were established: Vys / Regulatus Authority, Lucrest Rebellion, and Virtuasti.

This created a stronger political and social frame for future conflict.

### Next Era Inheritance

The next era now has a clearer starting point.

Lucrest, Arumis, the Virtuasti, rebellion, medicine, preservation, augmentation, and House Devaar all have cleaner paths forward.

## Design Takeaway

The structure protects causality and keeps later systems tied to earlier wounds.
