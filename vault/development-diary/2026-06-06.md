# 2026-06-06 — Erit World Development Diary

## Main Purpose

Today focused on structure before continuing worldbuilding.

The goal was to make the Erit World files easier to read, easier to expand, and more useful as a portfolio-facing design trail.

The work was not about adding random lore. It was about creating better containers for future lore and protecting causality as the world expanded.

## Main Discovery

The folder structure itself became part of the design method.

Each era needed a clear function so later worldbuilding would not spread sideways too quickly.

The active structure became:

```text
Founding -> Fracture -> Devaar Rise -> Erit Era
```

This gives the world a spine: hidden origin, family betrayal, Regulatus, city expansion, rebellion, institutions, and eventual movement toward House Devaar.

## Key Developments

### Portfolio Structure

The public-facing Erit worldbuilding material was reorganized so readers could move from polished overview to deeper process.

The intended reading path became:

```text
polished sample
-> deeper process trail
-> internal reference files
```

This makes the worldbuilding easier to present as narrative design work rather than a loose lore archive.

### Diary Split

The development diary was separated into dated entries.

This made the process easier to follow and kept the original combined diary from becoming the only entry point.

### Era Structure

The Erit World folder was organized around historical eras:

- Overview
- Founding Era
- Fracture Era
- Devaar Rise Era
- Erit Era

Each era has a dramatic function, core question, current focus, and constraint.

### Founding Era Cleanup

The Founding Era was tightened around Ecalus, Viriatus, House Ventari, and the murder sequence.

The strongest lock was:

```text
Era One ends when private murder becomes public history.
```

This connects Viriatus's private death to Regulatus, AVD, and the public memory controlled by House Ventari.

### Fracture Era Shape

The Fracture Era was given a checkpoint structure:

- 0 AVD
- 5 AVD
- 10 AVD
- 20 AVD
- 30 AVD
- 40 AVD

This kept the era readable and prevented the first generation from stretching too far.

The era tracks how Regulatus moves from declaration into institution, resistance, city conflict, and faction formation.

### Cities And Factions

The first major city pressures were established:

- Vys as the official center of Regulatus authority
- Lucrest as a fertile and difficult-to-reach rebel center
- Arumis as a port, mining, academic, and metallurgical future

By the end of the Fracture Era, three major forces exist:

- Vys / Regulatus Authority
- Lucrest Rebellion
- Virtuasti

## Constraints Found

The timeline should remain the spine.

New files should only be created when they anchor a major person, sequence, system, or repeated future confusion.

Names alone do not require files.

Current focus:

```text
Build the Fracture Era clearly enough that the next era can inherit it.
```

The next era should begin from clean inherited pressure:

- Lucrest under Regulatus by force
- Arumis under Regulatus by alliance
- Virtuasti surviving as an institution
- rebellion wounded but not erased
- Arumis prepared for later medicine, preservation, chips, augmentation, and House Devaar

## Design Takeaway

The folders are not just organization.

They protect causality.

The strongest current chain is:

```text
hidden origin
-> useful child
-> conquest
-> usefulness becoming love
-> family betrayal
-> Regulatus
-> Vys stabilization
-> city expansion
-> rebellion
-> institutions
-> faction triangle
-> time jump
```
