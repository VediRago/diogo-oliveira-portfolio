# Common Terms

Small vocabulary references for Erit's world.

## Servan

Servan are servants or service-bound workers within the old Roman-derived order.

In the Viriatus origin, Servan matter because they see what the powerful need hidden. They are close enough to witness House Ventari's private truth, but low-status enough to be disbelieved, blamed, punished, or erased.

Their role is not to form a separate faction. Their role is to show how truth becomes dangerous when it belongs to people without power.
