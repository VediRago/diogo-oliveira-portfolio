# Ecalus Ventari

Ecalus Ventari is the head of [House Ventari](house-ventari.md) during the discovery of [Viriatus](viriatus.md).

He begins by seeing Viriatus as a weapon, proof, and advantage that can help House Ventari rise beyond the other great houses.

Under Ecalus, Viriatus is sheltered, trained, and shaped into the force that helps overthrow the old Roman-derived order.

Over time, usefulness becomes love.

Near death, Ecalus begins seeing Viriatus less as an asset and more as the son he wished he had.

He dies before creating the next order.

After his death, [Ermerus Ventari](ermerus-ventari.md) takes the reins, and Viriatus's public meaning becomes dangerous.
