# House Ventari

House Ventari was one of the sixteen powerful houses of the old Roman-derived order. Influential but not dominant, the house sought to rise beyond the balance that kept the great houses in check.

Before the founding era hardened into conquest, the house still carried an older noble identity. That identity begins to fracture after the death of [Ecalus Ventari](ecalus-ventari.md)'s wife, who dies giving birth to [Ermerus Ventari](ermerus-ventari.md). Her death becomes one of the private wounds beneath the house's public transformation.

After discovering [Viriatus](viriatus.md), House Ventari hid his arrival, raised him, taught him, and shaped him into the force that would help overthrow the old order and conquer beyond it.

The house's history is shaped by Ecalus, Ermerus, [Rutare Ventari](rutare-ventari.md), [Ilunis Ventari](ilunis-ventari.md), and [Teresta Ventari](teresta-ventari.md), whose choices ultimately lead to the creation of [Regulatus](regulatus.md) and the beginning of the [AVD era](avd-calendar.md).

Although united publicly, each member carries different ambitions, wounds, and visions for what should come after Viriatus.

Publicly, House Ventari turns conquest into order and grief into civic legitimacy. Privately, that order is built through silence, family resentment, Viriatus's exploitation, and the conversion of gratitude into political control.

The later fracture that produces [Novacula](novacula.md) begins inside the same logic House Ventari uses to secure Regulatus: blood, legitimacy, controlled history, and imposed public meaning.

## Erit Note

The first letters of Ecalus, Rutare, Ilunis, and Teresta form **ERIT**.

In the original [NOEME](https://github.com/VediRago/Noeme) story, Erit is the name of one of the fragments, given to her by her mother as an homage and as a word meant to show pride in the family.

Erit means **to become**, derived from **Sera**, another fragment.
