# Novacula

Novacula begins from rejection inside the same house that created Regulatus.

After Ermerus Ventari dies, his disputed children lose protection inside House Ventari. Later family branches use blood legitimacy to remove them and their claim from secure power.

That rejection becomes the wound Novacula grows from.

Novacula does not begin as a simple rejection of meaning. It begins as a rejection of a meaning controlled by the people who cast them out.

Where Regulatus turns Viriatus's death into order, law, and public truth, Novacula turns exclusion into suspicion, rebellion, and the need to cut through deception.
